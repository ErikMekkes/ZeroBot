This rank is for those PvM elitists among us who want to be and stay at the highest level. The pvm specialist rank reqs are short but extremely challenging: Show that you meet the specialist tag criteria for a boss.

```PvM Specialist:```
- Must have passed one of the Specialist boss tags.
- Must have all the gear / PvM unlocks from advanced member and lower ranks.

See the <#755476321609580635> channel for specialist tag requirements for each boss.
